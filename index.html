This section represents the USD to Brazilian Real rate without any other tax from the exchange

This section represents the USD to Brazilian Real rate with the active order tax. A active order executes one or more passive order in the order book, meaning your order will be executed at the time of submittal.

Most of the time, active orders are more expensive than passive orders

This section represents the USD to Brazilian Real rate with the passive order tax. A passive order waits for one or more active order in the order book, meaning your order will be kept at the order book at the time of submittal.

Most of the time, passive orders are cheaper than active orders
